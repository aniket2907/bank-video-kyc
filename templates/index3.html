Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is KYC? Why is it necessary?

A: KYC stands for 'Know Your Customer'. It allows banks to verify customers' identities in order to prevent fraud, laundering and other malicious financial expoitations. It involves linking the customer's government ID with our database.

Q: How can I begin my KYC?

A: Once you receive the prompt from us to begin KYC, you can click on the included link to get started. Make sure to keep a relevant details handy!

Q: What should I do if I find myself stuck at any step in the process?

A: The ANX KYC process has been carefully curated to ensure a streamlined experience for users. At every step, instructions have been provided to aid customers.

Q: I am unable to get past the video verification step. Am I doing something wrong?

A: Our face detection system uses the haar cascade algorithm to verify that the submittee is human. In case the verification fails, retry and ensure that the video feed is open for 20-30 seconds for best results. Make sure you are in a well-lit environment, and don't forget to smile!

In case of further queries, feel free to contact ANX Support.
